Dog (Man's Best Friend)

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wold. It is derived fron an ancient, extinct wolf, and the modern wold is the dog's nearest living relative

  • Scientific Name: Canis Familiaris
  • Average Size: Varies by breed
  • Average Weight: Varies by breed
  • Habitat: Indoors - Various

Over the millennia, dogs became uniquely adapted to human behavior, and the human-canine bond has been a topic of frequent study. This influence on human society has given them the sobriquet of "man's best friend"
